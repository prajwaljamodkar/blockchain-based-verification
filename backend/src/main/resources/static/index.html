How It Works

A three-step process secured by cryptography and blockchain technology

①

Hash

The certificate PDF is run through SHA-256 to produce a unique 64-character fingerprint. Any change — even a single pixel — produces a completely different hash.

②

Record

The hash is recorded on the Ethereum blockchain via a smart contract. Once written, it cannot be altered or deleted — providing an immutable proof of issuance.

③

Verify

Anyone can verify a certificate by uploading it. The system re-computes the hash and checks it against the blockchain. A match means the document is authentic and unaltered.
